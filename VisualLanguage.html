Visual Language

Being a brand that revolves around communication, we understand the importance of different types of communication at various touchpoints. Our visual communication relies heavily on the elements that make up our visual language. They must stay in line with our overall identity, carrying a minimal, modern and dynamic look and feel.

Spokes Halo

This is one of our main graphic elements. It is derived from our icon. We will integrate this graphic element strategically in selected images/photography in order to further enhance the way in which we communicate and make the overall atmosphere more engaging. We need to ensure that the spaced out lines of this graphic element is always placed either near someone’s mouth or ear in order to convey that these people are either listening to or recording a Spoke.
